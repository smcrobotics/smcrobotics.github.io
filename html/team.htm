History

The SMC Robotics Club is a robotics club by and for the students of Santa Monica College.

The club was first founded in the early 2000s, but experienced a revival during spring 2019 by members Ebony Warren and Carlos Davila.

We seek to provide community college students with not only the opportunity to participate in exciting and educational robotics projects, but also to form personal and professional relationships with other likeminded students and aspiring engineers.
