Each year, our club competes in the VEX U Robotics competition.

We want to show the world that community colleges like us have just as much drive in engineering as other colleges and universities.

Learn about our work.

Our students seek to learn outside of the competition.

Using ROS as a medium and Turtlebot as a testbed, students learn cutting-edge robotics technologies like SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping), sensor fusion, navigation, etc.

Students drive progress.

We don't stop at simply purchasing parts from the shelf; we also design our own parts.

Using CAD and 3D printers, students fabricate custom robot parts to use in VEX or an independent project.

We are always on the lookout for new and exciting projects to pursue.

Feel free to reach out to us!
